Sanatan Saarthi – Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Sanatan Saarthi, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to agappsolutions25@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the email, include the email address you used to sign in with Google.
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm once your account and data have been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your name, email address, and profile picture (obtained via Google Sign-In).
  • Your meditation session history and session count.
  • Any bookmarks you have saved.
  • Japa Mala counters and other personal progress data.
  • Preferences and settings associated with your account.

After deletion, you will no longer be able to access the app with that account.

What Data Is Retained?

No personal data is retained after deletion. Anonymized usage statistics (not linked to you) may be kept for analytical purposes.
